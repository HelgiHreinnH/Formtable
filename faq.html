Product & Sizing

What makes Form Desk different from other office furniture? +

Three key differences: We eliminate the supplier maze with a single-source solution, every desk features our signature corner fillet design system, and we focus on brand personality over generic corporate furniture.

Instead of navigating complex supplier catalogs, you get three optimized desk sizes with consistent quality materials and Danish craftsmanship.

Ordering & Business

What's the minimum order quantity? +

No minimum order. Whether you need 1 desk for a startup or 100 for a growing company, we handle orders of all sizes.

Volume pricing kicks in at 10+ units, with additional discounts at 25+ and 50+ desks.

How long does delivery take? +

Standard delivery: 4-6 weeks from order confirmation to your office.

Rush orders: 2-3 weeks available for additional fee (subject to current production schedule).

All deliveries include white-glove service with professional assembly and packaging removal.

Do you ship internationally? +

EU shipping: Available to all EU countries with transparent pricing and no surprise customs fees.

Rest of world: Contact us for custom shipping quotes. We've successfully delivered to North America, Asia, and Australia.

Can I get samples or see the desk in person? +

Material samples: We send physical samples of all finishes and corner details free of charge.

Copenhagen showroom: Visit our studio during 3 Days of Design (June 2025) or schedule a private appointment.

AR preview: Use our mobile AR tool to place a full-size desk in your space instantly.

Sustainability & Quality

How sustainable are Form Desks? +

Materials: FSC certified timber, renewable linoleum, and recyclable metal components.

Production: Danish craftsmanship with minimal waste and local supplier networks.

Longevity: Built to last decades, not years. Our design and quality reduce furniture turnover.

What's the warranty coverage? +

10-year structural warranty on all frame components and height adjustment mechanisms.

5-year surface warranty against defects in materials and craftsmanship.

Lifetime design support: If you need replacement parts or want to modify your setup, we're here to help.

How do you ensure quality consistency? +

Single production facility ensures every desk meets identical standards.

Pre-delivery inspection of every unit before shipping.

Batch tracking: We can trace every component back to its source for quality control.
